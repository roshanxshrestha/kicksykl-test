Keep It Looking Sharp

Leather Care Tips

Keep it dry. Avoid prolonged exposure to water. If it gets wet, pat dry with a soft cloth and let it air-dry naturally — never use direct heat.

Store away from direct sunlight. Prolonged sun exposure can fade and dry out leather over time. A drawer or pouch works best.

Condition occasionally. A small amount of leather conditioner every few months keeps it supple and prevents cracking — especially in wallets and belts that flex daily.

Avoid overloading. Wallets and card holders last longer when not stretched beyond their intended capacity — it preserves shape and stitching.
