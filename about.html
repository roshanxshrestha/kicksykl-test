Our Story

Born From a Simple Belief

Every great brand starts with a simple frustration. For me, it was the realization that elevating your style in Nepal always seemed to come with an unfair "luxury tax." Whether I was hunting for the perfect pair of sneakers or a personalized leather wallet, the market forced a frustrating choice: settle for low-quality products or pay outrageously inflated prices.

I created Kicksy to shatter that compromise. To make this vision a reality, we operate exclusively as an online-based store. By cutting out expensive physical retail overhead, we can invest entirely in the quality of our products and pass those savings directly to you. And because great style shouldn't be limited by geography, we proudly deliver straight to your doorstep, anywhere in Nepal.

Today, our brand operates through two dedicated avenues, both built on the exact same foundation: giving you premium quality without the unreasonable price tag.

Kicksy Nepal: Step Into Premium Style

It all started with footwear. I quickly noticed that the prices for high-quality master copies of premium sneaker brands were artificially high. Driven by unnecessary markups, getting your hands on top-tier street style felt out of reach. Kicksy Nepal bridges that gap. We carefully source the finest master copies, ensuring you can rock the silhouettes you love without emptying your wallet.

Kicksy Leather: Bespoke Craftsmanship

The vision naturally expanded when I saw the exact same problem in the leather market. Finding a genuine, custom-made leather wallet and other accessories meant either dealing with rigid, mass-produced designs or paying a small fortune. Through Kicksy Leather, we handcraft and fully customize genuine leather essentials. We obsess over the details, allowing you to personalize your everyday carry with authentic craftsmanship at an honest, accessible price.

Whether you are lacing up a fresh pair of kicks or pulling out a custom leather wallet, you are joining a community that values smart choices, great style, and fair pricing.

— Roshan, Founder of Kicksy
